Box Shadow

Multiple Shadows

This is a "layer cake", in which the first shadow in the list is placed at the very top, and the last one is at the very bottom. That is, the higher a shadow is listed, the higher it is in the layers, and it will visually overlap all subsequent ones. This approach is ideal for very soft and beautiful shadows.

(For clarity, let's set different colors on the shadows)
